Let the journey begin...
This is where it all started. In 1985, when I was 5 years old, my dad sat me down in front of a good 'ole IBM 8088. The processor was clocked at a screaming 5Mhz, had a gigantic 20mb hard drive, and a nice 4 color monitor. Man, how times have changed.
I quickly began getting familiar with it and started off playing games like Qubert, Snakes, and Bricks. Later, I began programming in QBasic and wrote some basic text adventure games.
As I grew up, my knowledge of computers grew as well and I quickly found out that I was born to program....
Off to School
I started pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Clemson in 1998. My first programming class was in Java and I remember that the concepts were escaping me until one day, my final exam day. As I was taking the exam, suddenly a light-bulb went off in my head and everything the professor taught us that semester 'clicked'. I took a sigh of relief and then quickly breezed through the rest of the exam. I pulled an 'A' on the exam and an 'A' in the class and went on to finish my degree in 4 years.
